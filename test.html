Mandalorian

The Mandalorians were a clan-based cultural group that was comprised of members from multiple species all bound by a common culture, creed, and code.

They originated on the planet Mandalore in the galaxy's Outer Rim Territories and had a particularly important role in galactic history as legendary warriors against the Jedi.



From their homeworld, Mandalorians had flourished across Mandalorian Space and the galaxy at large, colonizing worlds such as Kalevala, Krownest, and Concord Dawn.
